    SKILL

    My Skill

    Front End Web Developer

    Implementing the visual elements and user interface of a website. This includes designing and coding the layout, navigation, and interactive features that users see and interact with directly in their web browsers. Front-end developers work closely with designers to bring the visual concepts to life, using technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. They focus on creating an engaging and user-friendly experience, ensuring that the website is not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and intuitive.

    System Analyst

    Involves analyzing the current systems, understanding the business requirements, and proposing solutions that involve technology to enhance efficiency or solve a particular problem. act as a bridge between business needs and the IT department, ensuring that the systems developed or implemented align with the organization's goals. Systems analysts also document requirements, design specifications, and collaborate with various stakeholders to ensure a smooth development and implementation process.

    UI/UX Design

    Creating the overall look and feel of a website or application to ensure a positive user experience. Work on creating wireframes, prototypes, and final designs.

    Project Manager

    Responsible for planning, organizing, and overseeing all aspects of a project to ensure it's completed successfully. This includes defining project goals, creating schedules, managing resources, and coordinating the efforts of the team. Act as the main point of communication between different stakeholders, making sure everyone is on the same page and addressing any challenges that arise. Their goal is to deliver the project on time, within budget, and with the expected level of quality. It's a bit like conducting a symphony to make sure all the instruments (or team members) are playing in harmony!

    IT Support

    providing assistance and resolving technical issues related to computer systems, software, and hardware. They help individuals or organizations with various IT-related problems, such as troubleshooting software issues, setting up and maintaining computer systems, configuring networks, and addressing hardware problems. Responsible for installing and updating software, ensuring system security, and offering guidance to users on how to use technology effectively.
